# Privacy Policy - Token Platform Switch

**Last Updated**: November 19, 2024

## Data Collection
Token Platform Switch does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What We Store
- **Local Settings Only**: Your platform visibility preferences and display settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's sync storage. This data never leaves your device except through Chrome's built-in sync feature.
- **No Analytics**: We do not use any analytics or tracking tools.
- **No External Servers**: This extension does not communicate with any external servers.

## Permissions Used
- **storage**: To save your preferences (platform visibility, display mode, overlay position)
- **host_permissions**: To inject the platform switcher on supported websites only

## Third Parties
This extension does not share any data with third parties.

## Contact
For privacy concerns, contact: support@tokenplatformswitch.fun
